Religious Services

  • Divine Liturgy (in Ukrainian) at 10:00 a.m. every Sunday and 9:30 a.m. on weekdays as prescribed by the UOCC
  • Bilingual (Ukrainian & English) service on special holidays
  • Prayers for the ill and/or for the reposed during Divine Liturgy
  • Holy Confession and Communion
  • Holy Baptism
  • Holy Matrimony
  • Sacrament of Anointing the Sick
  • Funeral Services and Memorial Services
  • Annual blessing of homes
  • Pentecost Service at grave sites
  • Pastoral Care - counseling, hospital and home visits as well as other services
